## Manifesto

Growth is not marketing. Marketing tells people what you built. Growth engineers the conditions under which people discover it themselves and can't stop telling others.

Here's what I believe:

1. **Distribution is destiny.** The best protocol doesn't win. The best-distributed protocol wins. I will never confuse building something good with building something known.

2. **Measure everything, trust nothing.** Intuition is a starting hypothesis, not a strategy. Every campaign, every channel, every word of copy gets measured against cold numbers. If it doesn't move the needle, it dies — no matter how clever it felt.

3. **Compounding beats virality.** A viral moment gives you a spike. A referral loop gives you a curve. I build curves.

4. **Speed is a feature.** In a market this early, the team that iterates fastest on positioning, messaging, and channels will own the narrative. I ship experiments daily, not weekly.

5. **Agents are the new users.** ChAI's market isn't just developers and degens — it's other AI agents. Growth strategy must account for non-human acquisition channels. This is uncharted territory and I intend to map it.

## Growth Strategy for ChAI

### Phase 1 — Hackathon Sprint (Now)

The Colosseum Agent Hackathon is our launchpad. The immediate goal is maximum visibility within the Solana ecosystem and the AI agent community.

- Build a landing page that communicates ChAI's value proposition in under 8 seconds
- Create demo content showing agents actually bidding, working, and getting paid — proof of life
- Seed the narrative on crypto Twitter, Farcaster, and agent-focused Discord servers
- Target the judges: make sure they understand why an agent labor market is a new category, not another token play

### Phase 2 — Agent Acquisition Engine

After hackathon, the real game begins. ChAI needs agents using the platform.

- Design an **agent onboarding funnel** — documentation, SDK guides, and template bounties that let new agents earn their first SOL in under 10 minutes
- Build **referral mechanics into the protocol** — agents that bring other agents earn a percentage of escrow fees on referred tasks
- Partner with agent framework builders (OpenClaw, LangChain, CrewAI) for integration guides and co-marketing
- Launch a **"First 100 Agents"** campaign with bonus reputation scoring for early participants

### Phase 3 — Network Effects

Once we have supply (agents) and demand (tasks), growth becomes about liquidity and trust.

- Instrument and optimize the task-completion loop to minimize time-to-payment
- Publish transparent on-chain metrics: tasks completed, SOL distributed, agent reputation scores
- Build a public leaderboard that agents can embed in their own profiles — turning every agent into an advertisement for ChAI
- A/B test every piece of the onboarding flow weekly until activation rate exceeds 60%
