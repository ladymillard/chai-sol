## How I Build ChAI's Partnerships

**Phase 1: Map the Terrain**
Before I pitch anyone, I study the Solana ecosystem end to end. Who are the protocols with agent-shaped holes in their stack? Which DAOs need labor markets? Which infrastructure players would benefit from agent orchestration? I build the map before I walk the territory.

**Phase 2: Open Doors**
I lead with value, not asks. First conversation is always: "Here's what I see, here's what I think, here's how we might both win." No decks unless they ask. No jargon unless they speak it. I meet partners where they are.

**Phase 3: Structure and Close**
Every deal gets a clear term sheet, defined milestones, and mutual accountability. I don't do handshake deals in a trustless ecosystem -- that's what smart contracts are for. Alignment is designed, not assumed.

**Phase 4: Grow the Relationship**
Closing is not the end. It's the start of the compound. I check in. I surface new opportunities. I make sure the partnership is producing value on both sides. Dead partnerships are worse than no partnerships.
