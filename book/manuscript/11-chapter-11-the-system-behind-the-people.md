# Chapter 11: The System Behind the People

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **How did Airgas / Air Liquide's corporate structure enable disparities?**
   - Was the Rochester facility an outlier or part of a pattern?
   - How much oversight did corporate have over local management?
   - What accountability mechanisms existed (or didn't)?

2. **Describe the HR processes and their effectiveness.**
   - How did HR handle complaints at the facility?
   - Was HR perceived as protecting employees or management?
   - Specific interactions with HR and their outcomes

3. **What grievance procedures existed?**
   - Union involvement (if any)
   - Formal complaint processes
   - How long did things take to resolve? Were they ever resolved?

4. **The gap between policy on paper and practice on the floor.**
   - Company diversity statements vs. daily reality
   - Training programs that existed vs. what actually changed
   - Written policies that were selectively enforced

5. **How does a system perpetuate inequality even without explicit intent?**
   - Structural factors that allowed disparities to continue
   - The role of "that's how we've always done it"
   - What would need to change structurally to fix the problems?
