# Chapter 4: Different Rules

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **Describe the first time you noticed disparate treatment.**
   - What happened? Who was involved?
   - How did it make you feel?
   - Did you say anything at the time?

2. **Give specific examples of unequal policy enforcement.**
   - Attendance policies — applied differently?
   - Discipline — same infractions, different consequences?
   - Promotions or raises — who got them and why?

3. **Were there patterns in how Black vs. white employees were treated?**
   - Task assignments and workload distribution
   - Access to training or advancement opportunities
   - How mistakes were handled for different employees

4. **How did other employees react to these disparities?**
   - Did white coworkers notice or acknowledge it?
   - Did Black coworkers talk about it among themselves?
   - Was there a sense of "that's just how it is"?

5. **Document specific incidents with as much detail as possible.**
   - Dates, names, locations, witnesses
   - What was said and done
   - What the outcome was
