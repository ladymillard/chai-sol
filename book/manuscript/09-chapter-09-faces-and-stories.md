# Chapter 9: Faces and Stories

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **Profile 3-5 coworkers and their experiences.**
   - Who were they? What were their roles?
   - What did they go through at the facility?
   - How did their experiences mirror or differ from yours?

2. **Describe shared struggles and solidarity.**
   - Moments when employees supported each other
   - How did Black employees build community within the workplace?
   - Acts of kindness or courage from coworkers

3. **Tell the stories of those who left.**
   - Who quit and why?
   - Were there people who were pushed out?
   - What happened to them after they left?

4. **Were there white coworkers who were allies?**
   - People who recognized the disparities and acted
   - The risks they took and the consequences they faced
   - What made them different from the others?

5. **How do these stories connect to the larger pattern?**
   - Individual stories that illustrate systemic problems
   - The human cost behind the statistics
   - Why these stories need to be told
