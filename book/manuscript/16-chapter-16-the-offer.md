## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **Describe the decision to write the letter to Airgas / Air Liquide.**
   - Why offer them the chance to buy the manuscript?
   - What outcome were you hoping for?
   - Who helped you draft and send it?

2. **Walk through the letter itself.**
   - The three reasons: Competitive Risk, Client Relations, Reputational Management
   - Why these specific arguments?
   - What message were you really sending?

3. **What was the company's response (or lack thereof)?**
   - Did they acknowledge receiving the letter?
   - Any communication from their legal team?
   - How their response (or silence) made you feel

4. **What does this offer represent to you?**
   - Is it about money, accountability, recognition, or something else?
   - What would you do if they accepted?
   - What will you do if they don't?

5. **What does the future hold?**
   - Your plans for the manuscript regardless of their decision
   - Other steps you're considering
   - What justice looks like to you
