1. **What was your training like at Airgas / Air Liquide?**

2. **What were your early assignments?**

   - How did you feel about the work itself?

3. **Describe the facility operations.**

   - What were the safety protocols?

4. **How did you build relationships with coworkers?**
   - Who were the first people you connected with?
   - Were there social dynamics along racial lines from the start?
   - Any early friendships or mentorships?

5. **What did you learn about the culture in those first months?**
   - What unwritten rules did you pick up on?
   - How did people talk about management?
   - Were there any early warning signs you noticed?
