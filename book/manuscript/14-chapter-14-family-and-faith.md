# Chapter 14: Family and Faith

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **How did 19 years in a hostile workplace affect your family?**
   - Did you bring the stress home? How did it show?
   - How did your spouse/partner/children experience this with you?
   - Conversations you had (or didn't have) at home about work

2. **How did your family sustain you?**
   - Specific moments of support
   - How family gave you perspective
   - The role of home as a refuge

3. **What role did faith play in your journey?**
   - Church, community, spiritual practices
   - How faith helped you endure
   - Conversations with God, pastors, or fellow believers about your situation

4. **The human cost that doesn't show up in HR files.**
   - Missed moments, strained relationships, health impacts
   - The parts of the story that are invisible to the workplace
   - What you sacrificed that the company will never know about

5. **How did your community in Rochester support you?**
   - Friends, neighbors, community organizations
   - People outside the workplace who understood what you were going through
   - The broader Black community's experience with similar workplaces
