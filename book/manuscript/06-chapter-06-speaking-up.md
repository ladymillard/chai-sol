# Chapter 6: Speaking Up

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **When did you first try to address concerns formally?**
   - What prompted you to speak up?
   - Who did you go to first — supervisor, HR, union?
   - How did you prepare yourself?

2. **What was management's response?**
   - How did Bob Hewitt respond when issues were raised?
   - How did Bob Gross respond?
   - Were your concerns taken seriously or dismissed?

3. **What happened after you complained?**
   - Any retaliation — direct or subtle?
   - Changes in assignments, treatment, or relationships?
   - Did the situation improve, worsen, or stay the same?

4. **Did other employees also speak up?**
   - Were you alone or did others join you?
   - What happened to others who raised similar concerns?
   - Was there fear of retaliation among coworkers?

5. **What did you learn about "the system" from trying to use it?**
   - HR processes — did they work as intended?
   - Grievance procedures — were they fair?
   - Did you feel the system was designed to protect employees or the company?
