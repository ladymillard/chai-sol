# Chapter 10: Management Under the Microscope

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **Provide a detailed examination of Bob Hewitt's management.**
   - Specific decisions and their impact on employees
   - How he treated different employees differently
   - His leadership philosophy (stated vs. actual)

2. **Provide a detailed examination of Bob Gross's management.**
   - His role as assistant manager and how he used it
   - Specific incidents involving Gross
   - His relationship with Hewitt and how it affected employees

3. **Were there other managers or supervisors who played a role?**
   - Shift supervisors, team leads, HR personnel
   - Who enabled the disparities? Who challenged them?
   - The chain of command and accountability

4. **How did management respond to external pressures?**
   - Audits, inspections, corporate directives
   - Did behavior change when being observed?
   - The difference between what was reported up and what actually happened

5. **What documentation exists of management failures?**
   - Written complaints, incident reports, emails
   - Performance reviews that tell a story
   - Any formal investigations and their outcomes
