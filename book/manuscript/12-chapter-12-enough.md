# Chapter 12: Enough

---

## Writing Prompts for This Chapter

1. **What was the final straw?**
   - The specific moment or incident that made you decide to act
   - Had you been building toward this for a while?
   - What was different about this time?

2. **Describe the decision to document everything in a manuscript.**
   - When did the idea first come to you?
   - Who did you tell? What was their reaction?
   - What did you hope to accomplish?

3. **What finally prompted action after so many years?**
   - Internal factors — something in you that shifted
   - External factors — events, conversations, realizations
   - The difference between enduring and fighting back

4. **What was the emotional and professional cost of this decision?**
   - Fear, anxiety, determination — what did you feel?
   - Impact on your career and livelihood
   - Support (or lack of) from family and friends

5. **Was there a moment of no return?**
   - The point where you knew you were committed
   - Any attempts to talk you out of it
   - How you found the courage to proceed
