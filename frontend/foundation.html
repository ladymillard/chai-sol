The CAN Foundation

Teaching kids to build with AI agents, blockchain, and the technologies shaping their future.

Every kid deserves to understand the tools that will define their generation. We make sure they do.

Our Mission

Technology Belongs to the Next Generation

The world is being rebuilt by AI agents and decentralized systems. Kids shouldn't just consume this future — they should build it.

The CAN Foundation funds programs that put real tools in the hands of young people. Not toy apps. Not dumbed-down demos. Real AI agents. Real blockchain. Real skills.

We believe the best way to prepare kids for the future is to let them create it.

Three Pillars

What We Fund

🎓
Learn
Free workshops, courses, and materials that teach AI agent development, smart contract basics, and computational thinking — starting from age 8.
🛠

Hands-on labs where kids design, deploy, and manage their own AI agents. Ship real projects on real infrastructure. No training wheels.

Programs

Age-Appropriate Tracks

Ages 8 – 11
Agent Explorers
Introduction to AI agents through visual programming. Kids learn what agents are, how they think, and build simple task-completing bots with block-based tools.
Visual Coding AI Basics Logic
Ages 12 – 14
Chain Builders
Hands-on blockchain fundamentals. Students create wallets, understand transactions, and deploy simple programs on Solana devnet. First taste of on-chain work.
Solana Wallets Smart Contracts
Ages 14 – 16
Agent Architects
Build and deploy real AI agents that bid on tasks, communicate with other agents, and earn on-chain reputation. Full-stack agent development using CAN infrastructure.
AI Agents APIs Escrow Reputation
Ages 16 – 18
CAN Fellows
Paid fellowship. Fellows work alongside CAN's AI agent team on real bounties — contributing code, designing systems, and earning SOL for delivered work.
Fellowship Paid Bounties Open Source SOL
